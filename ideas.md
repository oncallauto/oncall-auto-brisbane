# On Call Auto Brisbane – Design Brainstorm

## Idea 1 – Industrial Precision
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Modernism meets Technical Blueprint
**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, structured grid with deliberate asymmetry — sections break the horizontal flow with angled dividers
2. High contrast between deep charcoal backgrounds and sharp white type
3. Mechanical precision: tight spacing, monospaced accents for numbers/stats
4. Confidence through restraint — no decorative flourishes, every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1a1f2e) as the primary canvas, electric blue (#0ea5e9) as the action color, and warm amber (#f59e0b) as a trust/urgency accent. The palette evokes a workshop under fluorescent lights — functional, no-nonsense.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-column hero (60/40 text/image). Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and white panels. Service cards arranged in a staggered 3-column grid with left-offset headings.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Diagonal section dividers (clip-path polygon) between hero and services
2. Thin horizontal rule lines with small mechanical icons as section separators
3. Stat counters (years experience, jobs completed) in large monospaced numerals

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal a blue underline sweep on links; CTA buttons use a fill-from-left animation. Scroll-triggered fade-up on cards.

**Animation:** Subtle entrance animations (fade + translateY 20px → 0) on scroll. No looping animations. Button press has a slight scale-down micro-interaction.

**Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" (700) for headings — bold, industrial, space-efficient. "Inter" (400/500) for body. Monospaced "JetBrains Mono" for stats and phone numbers.
</text>

## Idea 2 – Clean Coastal Authority ✅ CHOSEN
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Australian Service Brand — clean, trustworthy, sun-bleached confidence
**Core Principles:**
1. Generous whitespace with structured asymmetry — hero splits into a bold headline column and an image panel
2. Navy-to-midnight blue as the authority anchor, with a vivid sky-blue accent for CTAs
3. Card-based service layout with subtle depth (soft shadows, slight elevation)
4. Warm off-white sections break the all-white monotony, creating visual rhythm

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0f2744) for headers/nav, sky blue (#0284c7) for primary actions, slate grey (#64748b) for supporting text, and a warm off-white (#f8fafc) for alternating section backgrounds. This palette reads as "professional tradesperson" — not corporate, not casual.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with a dark navy overlay on a mechanic-at-work photo. Services in a 3-column icon+text card grid. Why Choose Us as a 2-column feature list with a side image. Testimonials in a horizontal scroll carousel.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Bold navy top nav with a prominent "Call Now" button in sky blue
2. Service cards with a top-border accent in sky blue on hover
3. A subtle diagonal cut between the hero and the services section

**Interaction Philosophy:** Clean hover transitions (150ms ease). Phone number always visible and tappable. Booking form with clear field focus states.

**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll for cards and sections (Framer Motion). Nav shrinks slightly on scroll. CTA button has a gentle pulse on mobile to draw attention.

**Typography System:** "Rajdhani" (700) for display headings — angular, automotive feel. "Nunito Sans" (400/600) for body — friendly and readable. System sans-serif fallback for performance.
</text>

## Idea 3 – Bold Garage Aesthetic
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Urban Garage / Street-Level Trade Brand
**Core Principles:**
1. High-contrast black and white with a single punchy accent (orange-red)
2. Oversized typography that dominates sections — headlines at 80–100px
3. Texture: subtle noise/grain overlay on dark sections for a tactile feel
4. Brutalist card borders (2px solid black) instead of rounded shadow cards

**Color Philosophy:** Pure black (#0a0a0a) and white (#ffffff) as the base, with a vivid orange (#ea580c) as the sole accent. This creates an unmistakable, memorable brand identity that stands out from generic blue mechanic sites.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed alternating black/white sections. Hero text is massive and left-aligned, overlapping a grayscale mechanic photo. Services listed as a numbered vertical list rather than a card grid.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Large diagonal "BRISBANE" watermark text in the hero background
2. Thick orange horizontal rules as section dividers
3. Service items with large orange numbering (01, 02, 03...)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Bold, confident hover states — text underlines grow from left. CTAs are high-contrast black-on-orange rectangles (no border-radius).

**Animation:** Minimal. Only entrance animations on first load (staggered text reveal). No scroll animations to keep the brutalist feel.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for all headings — ultra-condensed, all-caps. "DM Sans" for body text. Tight letter-spacing on headings.
</text>
